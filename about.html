The 10% Pledge

I've signed the Giving What We Can pledge to give at least 10% of my income to the most effective charities I can find. I'm a student right now, so the numbers are small. But I want to commit to this before life gets complicated and it becomes easier to keep putting it off.

I came across the idea through people in the EA community, and Natalie Cargill's TED talk made it click for me. Worth a watch if you're curious.
