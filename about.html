The 10% Pledge

I've taken the Giving What We Can pledge to donate at least 10% of my lifetime income to the most effective charities. As Natalie Cargill argues in her TED talk "How to Solve the World's Biggest Problems", if the top 1% gave 10% of their income, we could address most of the world's critical challenges: ending extreme poverty, reducing animal suffering, and safeguarding our collective future.

Yes, I'm a student on an internship salary right now. But I graduate soon, and I intend to give substantially. I'm inspired by researchers I've met in AI Governance and AI Safety who have made this same commitment. I can live well on less. I pledge to give 10% to whichever organisations can most effectively improve lives, now and in the years to come.
